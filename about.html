What We Believe

Humanity
We believe that all humanity is created in God's image, possessing inherent worth and dignity. Each person is deserving of love and respect, and we are called to support, uplift, and serve one another in a spirit of compassion.
God in The Person
We believe that God is a loving and personal Creator, who desires a relationship with each of us. Through Jesus Christ, we experience His grace, mercy, and guidance, inviting us to trust in Him for our lives and salvation.
Salvation
We believe that salvation is a gift from God, offered through faith in Jesus Christ. By accepting His grace, we are forgiven of our sins and granted eternal life, transforming our hearts and lives to reflect His love.
Grace
We believe that grace is God’s unmerited favor towards us, extending forgiveness and love despite our shortcomings. It empowers us to live transformed lives, encourages us to extend kindness to others, and serves as a reminder of His boundless compassion.
  • 1

    Faith in Jesus Christ: We believe that faith in Jesus Christ is the foundation of our relationship with God. Through His life, death, and resurrection, He offers forgiveness and eternal life to all who believe. Accepting Jesus as our Savior transforms our hearts and lives, guiding us in love, grace, and purpose. Our faith empowers us to live out His teachings and share His love with others.
  • 2

    The Bible as the Inspired Word of God: We hold that the Bible is the inspired and infallible Word of God, serving as our ultimate guide for faith and practice. Its teachings reveal God’s character, His plans for humanity, and the path to salvation through Christ. We encourage regular reading, study, and application of Scripture in our daily lives, believing it equips us to grow spiritually and navigate life's challenges.
  • 3

    The Importance of Community: We believe that being part of a community is vital to our spiritual growth and well-being. Our church is a place where members support one another through prayer, fellowship, and service. We foster an environment of love and acceptance, encouraging everyone to share their gifts and experiences. Together, we celebrate life’s joys and navigate challenges, reflecting the love of Christ in our relationships.
  • 4

    The Call to Serve: We believe that serving others is a fundamental expression of our faith. Inspired by Jesus’ example, we are called to demonstrate God’s love through acts of kindness and outreach. Whether through local missions, community service, or global initiatives, we strive to meet the needs of those around us. Our commitment to serving empowers us to make a positive impact and share the hope of the Gospel.

Our Activities

Here is a list of activities that we carry out

Worship Services: Weekly gatherings where congregants come together to praise, sing, and hear scripture. Services often include prayers, sermons, and communion, creating a shared experience of spiritual connection and reflection.
Bible Study: Group sessions focused on exploring scripture in depth. Participants discuss interpretations, share insights, and apply teachings to daily life, fostering a deeper understanding of faith and community bonding.
Youth Ministry: Programs designed to engage and mentor young people in their faith journey. Activities include Bible studies, retreats, and community service, fostering spiritual growth and building lasting friendships.

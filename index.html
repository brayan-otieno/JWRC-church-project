Meet Our Pastors Who Share God’s Love

REV. Caroline Nyagechi
Meet Reverend Caroline Nyagechi, who is an inspirational and compassionate leader, known for her empowering spirit and unwavering faith. With her wisdom and charisma, she nurtures our community, encouraging us to grow spiritually. Her dedication and courage shine through in her innovative approach to ministry, making her a humble yet powerful presence. Always supportive and joyful, she engages with us thoughtfully, embodying resilience and authenticity that resonates deeply. Her visionary outlook guides us, fostering a grounded and inclusive environment for all.
Pst. Douglas Kigo
Meet Pastor Douglas Kigo is deeply devoted and inspiring leader, whose wisdom and compassion shine through in everything he does. He fosters a strong sense of community, encouraging us to grow in our faith while reaching out to those in need. His integrity and humility create a welcoming atmosphere, guiding us all on our spiritual journeys together.
Brother Humphrey Odaba
Meet Brother Humphrey Odaba who is a passionate and uplifting presence in our community. His commitment to service and outreach reflects his deep faith and compassion. With a genuine heart, he fosters fellowship among members, encouraging spiritual growth and connection. His insightful teachings and supportive nature inspire us all to live out our faith with love and purpose.
I’m New Here
Welcome to our church! We’re thrilled to have you here. Whether you’re exploring faith for the first time or looking for a new spiritual home, we want to make your experience as welcoming and meaningful as possible.
Care Ministries
Care ministries at our church are dedicated to nurturing the spiritual, emotional, and physical well-being of our congregation and community. With a mission to provide support during life’s challenges, our ministries include visitation programs for the sick and elderly, support groups for grief and addiction, and crisis assistance for families in need.
Prayer Request
At our church, we believe in the power of prayer and its ability to bring comfort, healing, and hope. If you have a prayer request, we invite you to share it with us. Whether you’re facing personal struggles, health challenges, or need guidance in your life, our prayer team is here to support you.
You can submit your prayer request through our website, by phone, or in person. All requests are treated with confidentiality and compassion. Our dedicated prayer team meets regularly to lift up the needs of our congregation and community in prayer.
Audio Podcasts
Our church offers audio podcasts to enrich your spiritual journey and deepen your faith. Each episode features inspiring sermons, discussions on biblical topics, and testimonies from members of our community.
Podcasts are a great way to stay connected, whether you’re at home, commuting, or enjoying a walk. You’ll find practical insights and encouragement to help you navigate life’s challenges with a faith-centered perspective.
